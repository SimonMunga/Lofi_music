About

Shawn, one of my good friends introduced me to Lofi music back in 2018 and since then I have been really hooked.

One thing I love about Lofi music is how it manages to keep me calm on days when I am feeling low or on days when I just want to listen to laid back tunes or even study.

Because of my love for lofi music, I decided to share a mix by Lofi Girl (on YouTube) that I really enjoy and thought it would be awesome to create a webiste using html and css instead of just sharing it on social media.

If you love what I did, please give me feedback by sending an email to samthedev17@gmail.com or if you know me personally, shoot me a text!

Enjoy :)
